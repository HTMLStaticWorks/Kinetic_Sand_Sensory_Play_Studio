Materials

Kinetic Sand is 98% ultra-fine sand and 2% magic (polymers). It sticks only to itself, meaning it feels like wet sand but never dries out and leaves your hands completely clean. It's non-toxic, gluten-free, and wheat-free.

Our studio is designed for explorers of all ages! While our primary workshops are tailored for ages 3 and up, we have specific sensory zones safe for toddlers and calming adult-only sessions for mindful relaxation.
